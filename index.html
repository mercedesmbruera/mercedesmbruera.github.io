Mercedes Martinez Bruera

Linguistics PhD. Candidate at Goethe University Frankfurt

I am a third year PhD student currently working on the relationship between language processing and diachronic change. I am interested in understanding if cognitive factors play a role in why a language devolops in a specific way. I am also interested in the reasons why heritage speakers may be different from monolingual speakers. To do this I use different psycolinguisitc methods to evaluate language processing and production
