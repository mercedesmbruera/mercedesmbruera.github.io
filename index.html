Mercedes Martinez Bruera

Linguistics PhD. Candidate at Goethe University Frankfurt

I am a second year PhD student currently working on the relationship between language processing and diachronic change. I investigate where and why heritage speaker grammars differ from monolingual grammars. To do this I use different psycolinguistics methods, to evaluate language processing and production.
